Pizza Recipe

Description

This dish will be a traditional pepporoni pizza. It will contain pizza dough, sauce, cheese, and pepporonis.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepporonis

Steps

  1. Roll out pizza dough into desired shape and size.
  2. Spread sauce over pizza dough.
  3. Sprinkle cheese on top of the sauce.
  4. Put pepporonis on top of the cheese.
  5. Put pizza in the oven to cook.
  6. Take pizza out of the oven and enjoy!
