Cereal Recipe

Description

This is a very basic dish that anyone can easily make! All we will need is your favorite cereal, some milk, a bowl, and a spoon!

Ingredients

  • Cereal
  • Milk
  • Bowl
  • Spoon

Steps

  1. Grab a bowl out of your cabinet.
  2. Pour the cereal out of the box into the bowl.
  3. Pour the milk into the bowl after the cereal.
  4. Grab a spoon and dig in!
