PBJ Recipe

Description

This simple sandwich is one that can be made very quickly and is a very convient dish to be taken on the go with you!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Grab two slices of bread and set them side by side.
  2. Spread some peanut butter over one slice of bread.
  3. Spread some jelly over the other slice of bread.
  4. Lay one piece of bread on top of the other, mushing the peanut butter and jelly together.
